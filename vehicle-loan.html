Vehicle Loan

About

For over two and a half decades, Anchal Leasing Private Limited has been supporting entrepreneurs to realize their dreams by financing vehicles. We offer loans for heavy and light commercial vehicles, multi-utility vehicles, tractors and recovery cranes.

Eligibility

The general factors taken into account while determining the eligibility of loan against property are listed below

  1. Minimum Age : 25 Years to Maximum Age : 60 Years (at the time of maturity of loan)
  2. Salaried and Self Employed individuals
  3. The company should have been in Business for minimum 3 years
  4. More than ₹ 25 Lacs in annual turnover
  5. India-based. For the time being, business must be based in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Pune or Chennai

Documents Required

  1. KYC proof
    • Pan card copy of promoters/directors & guarantor
    • Address proof (e bill copy) of residence
    • Business registration copy
    • Business address proof
  2. Last 3 years’ balance sheet; P&L with audit report
  3. 12 months’ bank statement
  4. Valid Identity Proof - as per RBI norms
    A valid Identity Proof will be any one of the following:
    • Passport
    • Driving License
    • Voter ID Card
    • Aadhar Card
    • PAN Card
  5. Valid Address Proof - as per RBI norms
    A valid Address Proof will be any one of the following:
      • Passport
      • Driving License
      • Ration Card
      • Gas Bill with Gas book
  6. Recent Passport size colors photographs
  7. Income Proof Documents
  8. Bank Statement for last 6 months
  9. Post Dated Cheques and Electronic Clearing Mandates
  10. Legal Documents as stipulated by Anchal leasing private limited
  11. Duly endorsed Vehicle Registration Certificate on receipt for New Vehicles
  12. Duly endorsed Vehicle Registration Certificate before sanction for Used Vehicles
  13. Any other document as advised by Anchal Leasing Private Limited
